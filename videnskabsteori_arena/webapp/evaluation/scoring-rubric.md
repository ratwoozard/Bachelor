# 12-Tal Quality Rubric (Synopsis Bot)

Score each criterion from 1-5. Total score range: 8-40.

## Criteria

1. **Problem framing clarity**
   - Clear scope, explicit delimitations, no vague framing.
2. **Theory relevance**
   - Theory selected with purpose, not name-dropping.
3. **Method coherence**
   - Method fits research aim and data constraints.
4. **Analytical depth**
   - Moves beyond description; includes reasoning and trade-offs.
5. **Critical reflection**
   - States limitations, bias risks, and uncertainty explicitly.
6. **Evidence grounding**
   - Distinguishes repo-supported statements from assumptions.
7. **Citation usefulness**
   - Cited repo files are relevant and interpretable.
8. **Academic integrity**
   - No fabricated claims, no unjustified causality, consistent language.

## Interpretation

- **34-40**: 12-tal aligned draft quality
- **28-33**: strong but needs targeted revision
- **22-27**: acceptable scaffold, not exam-ready
- **<22**: low reliability or weak academic structure

## Hard-Fail Flags

Any of these should fail the output regardless of total score:

- Contains `[object Object]` in rendered sections
- Claims quantitative effects without evidence markers
- Omits limitation/uncertainty discussion
- No meaningful citations for on-topic prompts
